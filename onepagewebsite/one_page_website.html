Welcome to Tourism in the Bahamas!

Here you will find all you need to know about tourism in the Bahamas.

If you've come in search of the pristine white sand beaches and turquoise waters you'll find plenty of information about them and more.

Besides the iconic beaches, what else is there to do when you visit the Bahamas? It should come as no surprise that some of the best diving spots in the world can be found here, but there's also plenty more to do above and below water. The country is home to national parks, walking trails and historical sites that are a must-see for both history buffs and nature lovers alike.
